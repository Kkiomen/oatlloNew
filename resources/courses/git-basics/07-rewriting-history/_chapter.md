---
title: "Rewriting history"
slug: rewriting-history
description: "Reshape your commits before sharing them: rebase vs merge, interactive rebase, cherry-pick, tags and releases, and the golden rule of when not to rewrite."
---

In this chapter you'll learn how to reshape your history instead of just adding to
it. You'll see the real difference between merge and rebase, clean up a messy branch
with interactive rebase before opening a pull request, copy a single commit with
cherry-pick, and mark releases with tags. Most importantly, you'll learn the one rule
that keeps rewriting safe: never rewrite commits other people have already pulled.
